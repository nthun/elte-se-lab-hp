title: "Posters and presentations at the Researchers' Forum"
date: "2026-03-25"
---

On the 25th of March, Researchers' Forum was held at ELTE PPK where most of our lab members presented their research. 

<!--more-->
Our lab members, Mercédesz Tóth, Dorottya Zorka Csala, Máté Nyírő-Kovács, Anna Viola Sándor and Donát Jakus presented their results with posters. 

Mercédesz Tóth poster was entitled Caffeine and interoception. Dorka and Máté's poster had a slightly different topic: developing a self-report caffeine sensitivity scale. Donát Jakus explored psychological variables in caffeine sensitivity with machine learning, while Anna's poster was about detecting and evaluating verbal aggression with AI models. 

Two of our senior lab members, Levente Rónai and Tamás Nagy also held a short speech about their latest results. Levente talked about emotions in everyday life, while Tamás presented his new data blinding R package, Vazul. 
The forum provided a nice opportunity to share the lab's results and to get to know other research groups and their ongoing projects. 

Congratulations to all participants!
